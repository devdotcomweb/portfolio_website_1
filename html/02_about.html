About Me

Why did I start learning to code?

Challenges give me the most motivation. The beginning of learning was a real challenge because I couldn't find anyone amongst my friends who knows about coding, I did and do everything myself. I experimented a lot with different instructors and teaching methods until I found what suits me.

How did I learn to code?

The way I learn the most is by first learning the material by listening to the course and taking notes. Then I print code for a simple project. I am trying to understand this code. I reach for the documentation. Finally, I try to rewrite this code myself. Usually I am not able to do it the first time. But in the second and third time it is possible

What am I working on now?

It's likely that as you read, I'm learning next.js and maybe even writing projects. I am familiar with this framework and I like it very much because it is elegant and relatively easy to learn.

Why do I want to become a web developer?

The longer I learn to code, the more I like doing it. Each completed project gives me a lot of satisfaction. I like to learn new things at work and thus develop myself and thus have a positive impact on the company in which I work.
